Before you contribute

BC's Local Elections Campaign Financing Act requires the following. We have to ask, and you have to qualify.

Contribution Rules
  • BC residents only. You must be a resident of British Columbia using your own personal funds.
  • Canadian citizen or permanent resident. No minimum age applies.
  • No businesses, organizations, or unions. Only individuals can contribute to local BC campaigns.
  • Annual limit set by Elections BC. If you contribute by credit card, processing fees count toward this limit.
  • Full legal name and residential address required. This information is required for campaign finance reporting.
  • Contributions are not tax-deductible. This is a political contribution, not a charitable donation.
Source: Elections BC - Local Elections Campaign Financing
