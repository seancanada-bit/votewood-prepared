"The best decisions come from the fullest conversations."

Parksville's governance affects every resident - homeowners and renters, business owners and retirees, young families and long-time neighbours. When the data is clear and the door is open, respectful disagreement becomes productive planning.

That's what prepared governance looks like: not one person with all the answers, but a whole community with all the information.

About this project

Every number on this site comes from public sources: BC Government financial reports, Statistics Canada, Environment Canada, the City of Parksville, and local journalism. Nothing here is secret - it's just been made easier to find and understand.

This is what a campaign built on transparency looks like. Not promises about what might happen, but data about what has happened and what it means for our future.
